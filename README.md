🏏 IPL Analysis Dashboard (2008–2025)

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Indian Premier League (IPL) data from 2008 to 2025 to uncover team performance, player achievements, and tournament trends.

🔍 Key Highlights

Season-wise winners & runner-ups

Dynamic points table & team standings

Orange Cap & Purple Cap analysis

Boundary insights (Total 4s & 6s)

Venue, match & season KPIs

📊 KPIs & DAX Measures

Total Matches, Teams & Venues

Total Sixes & Fours

Centuries & Half-Centuries

Points calculation & ranking logic

Season-level performance metrics

Implemented DAX measures for dynamic KPIs, rankings, and season filters to enable real-time insights.
